#### **Squidward Tentacles** - **Species**: Octopus - **Personality**: Grumpy, sarcastic, and often disdainful of others. - **Occupation**: Cashier at the Krusty Krab. - **Key Traits**: Squidward’s personality is in stark contrast to SpongeBob’s enthusiasm. Despite his negativity and frustration with his life, Squidward often ends up reluctantly involved in SpongeBob’s adventures. #### **Mr. Krabs** - **Species**: Crab - **Personality**: Greedy, money-driven, and a bit cheap. - **Occupation**: Owner of the Krusty Krab. - **Key Traits**: Mr. Krabs is obsessed with making money and runs the Krusty Krab with an iron claw. While he’s frequently shown as selfish and stingy, he has a soft spot for his daughter, Pearl, and occasionally displays moments of compassion. #### **Sandy Cheeks** - **Species**: Squirrel - **Personality**: Intelligent, strong, and adventurous. - **Occupation**: Scientist and inventor. - **Key Traits**: Sandy is one of the few land creatures in Bikini Bottom, and she lives in a special air-filled dome. She’s a skilled martial artist and often serves as a voice of reason among her more whimsical friends. Sandy’s Texan roots and scientific expertise make her a dynamic character in the show. #### **Plankton** - **Species**: Plankton - **Personality**: Evil, devious, and constantly scheming. - **Occupation**: Owner of the rival **Chum Bucket** restaurant. - **Key Traits**: Plankton is the main antagonist of the series. His lifelong goal is to steal the secret recipe for the Krabby Patty, the signature dish at the Krusty Krab. Despite his evil intentions, Plankton is often portrayed as comically ineffective, and his plans usually end in failure. ### **Setting: Bikini Bottom** **Bikini Bottom** is the fictional underwater city where the majority of the show’s action takes place. It’s an imaginative and whimsical world that combines traditional sea life with human-like elements. The city is full of anthropomorphic sea creatures living in underwater versions of real-world locations, such as the Krusty Krab, the Chum Bucket, and various quirky locations like **Jellyfish Fields**, **Goo Lagoon**, and **Rock Bottom**. The settings in **Bikini Bottom** are designed to be both familiar and surreal. The characters live in homes made from common objects, like a pineapple, a rock, and a hollowed-out trash can. However, the show is not just about comedy.
